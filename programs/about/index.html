About

Welcome to Fair Weather Friend!

Bringing you the weather from your friendly, neighborhood weather nerds at the Citizen Weather Observer Program in a nostalgic package!

This weather app aims to replicate The Weather Channel's WeatherSTAR 4000 display of the early 90's in a Windows 98 inspired UI

Data Sources

Weather observations from CWOP via the Synoptic Data API and the National Weather Service's National Digital Forecast Database

Weather alerts from the NWS MapClick API

Weather forecasting from the National Blend of Models via NOAA's NOMADS

Geodata from Google Maps and OpenStreetMap
